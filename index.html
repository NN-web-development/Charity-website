MISSION

Our mission is to contribute to the making of a just society by living the dream of the founder Maliviwe Cekiso, and convening dialogue on critical social issues.


VISION

Our vision is a just society, one which learns from its pasts and listens to all its voices whether young or old Having open dialogues about issues on all media platforms.
